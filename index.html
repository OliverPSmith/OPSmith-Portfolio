A little bit about me

 
Hi.
My name is Oliver.
And thanks for checking out my Portfolio. I am a self-taught web developer based in London / Surrey. Focused mainly on the front-end side of things, where I feel my developement has come a long way, and I now feel that I excel at making user friendly projects with easy on the eye aesthetics.

The languages and skills demonstrated in the projects and portfolio are mainly HTML, CSS, and Javascript. However I am currently spending time learning the React framework, amongst other things, to be more versed and rounded in the web development world and industry.

I am currently working in the retail industry, but since getting started with learning the programming languages mentioned above, I can honestly say I have never found an industry or career path that I am more passionate or driven to being succesful within. The pleasure of seeing your projects come to life, working and functioning properly, and looking good while doing so (with each project's CSS and styling looking better than the last), is an unparelled feeling. One of which I'm sure developers all over the world share.
